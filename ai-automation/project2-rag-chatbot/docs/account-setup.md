# Account Setup and Security

To create an account, visit app.company.com/signup and enter your email address. You will receive a verification email within 5 minutes. Check your spam folder if you do not see it.

Two-factor authentication (2FA) is available and recommended for all accounts. Go to Settings, then Security, then Enable 2FA. We support authenticator apps like Google Authenticator and Authy. SMS-based 2FA is not supported due to security concerns.

Password requirements: minimum 12 characters, at least one uppercase letter, one number, and one special character. Passwords expire every 90 days. You cannot reuse your last 5 passwords.

If you are locked out of your account, use the Account Recovery form at app.company.com/recover. You will need to verify your identity with the email address on file.
