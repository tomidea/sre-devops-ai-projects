# Refund Policy

All subscription plans come with a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you are not satisfied with your purchase, you can request a full refund within 30 days of your initial purchase date.

After the 30-day period, refunds are prorated based on remaining time in your billing cycle. Annual plans receive a prorated refund minus a 10% early termination fee.

To request a refund, contact our billing team at billing@company.com or submit a request through the Help Center. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days and returned to the original payment method.

Refunds are not available for add-on features purchased separately, custom enterprise contracts, or accounts that have violated our Terms of Service.
